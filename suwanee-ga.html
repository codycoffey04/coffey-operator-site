Weather & Flood Considerations

Properties near Suwanee Creek and the Chattahoochee River watershed face elevated flood risks. The greenway areas, while beautiful, can overflow during heavy rains typical of North Georgia storms. Standard homeowners policies exclude flood damage, making separate flood insurance critical for properties in flood-prone areas.

Recommended Coverage Options

  • Wind & Hail Protection: Essential for North Georgia's severe thunderstorm season.
  • Water Backup Coverage: Critical for homes with basements, common in Forsyth County.
  • Replacement Cost Coverage: Important given Suwanee's rising property values.
  • Identity Theft Protection: Valuable in this tech-savvy suburban community.

State Minimums vs. Recommended

Georgia requires minimum liability coverage of 25/50/25. However, with heavy traffic on Lawrenceville-Suwanee Road and McGinnis Ferry Road, most Suwanee residents opt for 100/300/100 or umbrella policies. Uninsured motorist coverage is highly recommended given the area's busy commuter traffic.
