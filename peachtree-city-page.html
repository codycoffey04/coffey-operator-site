Frequently Asked Questions - Peachtree City Insurance

Do I need insurance for my golf cart in Peachtree City? +

While Georgia doesn't require liability insurance for golf carts, we strongly recommend it. Peachtree City has over 10,000 registered golf carts sharing paths with pedestrians and crossing roads. Golf cart insurance typically costs $100-200 per year and covers:

  • Liability for injuries or property damage
  • Physical damage to your cart
  • Medical payments
  • Uninsured motorist protection
What's the minimum auto insurance required in Georgia? +

Georgia requires minimum liability coverage of 25/50/25, which means:

  • $25,000 bodily injury per person
  • $50,000 bodily injury per accident
  • $25,000 property damage

However, most Peachtree City residents choose higher limits (100/300/100) for better protection, especially given our community's higher property values.

Do I need flood insurance in Peachtree City? +

It depends on your location. Properties near Lake Peachtree, Lake Kedron, or Flat Creek may benefit from flood coverage. Standard homeowners policies don't cover flood damage. We can review FEMA flood maps for your specific address and recommend appropriate coverage.

How much can I save by bundling in 30269? +

Peachtree City residents typically save 15-25% by bundling auto and home insurance. The average bundle savings is $469 per year, but families with multiple vehicles, golf carts, and higher-value homes often save even more. Your exact savings depend on your specific situation.
