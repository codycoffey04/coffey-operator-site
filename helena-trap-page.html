Frequently Asked Questions - Helena Insurance

What's the minimum auto insurance required in Alabama? +

Alabama requires minimum liability coverage of 25/50/25, which means:

  • $25,000 bodily injury per person
  • $50,000 bodily injury per accident
  • $25,000 property damage

However, most Helena residents choose higher limits (100/300/50) for better protection, especially with increasing medical costs and newer vehicles in our community.

Do I need flood insurance in Helena? +

It depends on your location. Properties near Buck Creek or the Cahaba River may require or benefit from flood coverage. Standard homeowners policies don't cover flood damage. Even if you're not in a designated flood zone, Helena's heavy rains can cause water backup issues. We recommend discussing your specific property location with our agents.
