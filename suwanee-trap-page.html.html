Frequently Asked Questions - Suwanee Insurance

What's the minimum auto insurance required in Georgia? +

Georgia requires minimum liability coverage of 25/50/25, which means:

  • $25,000 bodily injury per person
  • $50,000 bodily injury per accident
  • $25,000 property damage

However, most Suwanee residents choose higher limits (100/300/100) for better protection, especially with increasing medical costs and vehicle values in Gwinnett County.

While Suwanee is not in a high-risk flood zone, properties near Suwanee Creek or in low-lying areas may benefit from flood coverage. Standard homeowners policies don't cover flood damage.
