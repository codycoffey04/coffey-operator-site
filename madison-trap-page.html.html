Frequently Asked Questions - Madison Insurance

What's the minimum auto insurance required in Alabama? +

Alabama requires minimum liability coverage of 25/50/25, which means:

  • $25,000 bodily injury per person
  • $50,000 bodily injury per accident
  • $25,000 property damage

However, most Madison residents choose higher limits (100/300/100 or 250/500/250) for better protection, especially given our area's higher income levels and property values.

Do I need flood insurance in Madison, AL? +

While Madison isn't in a high-risk flood zone, properties near Limestone Creek, Indian Creek, or in newer developments with modified drainage should consider flood coverage. Standard homeowners policies don't cover flood damage, and Madison has experienced flooding during heavy rain events.

What factors affect my insurance rates in Madison? +

Several local factors impact your rates:

  • Distance from fire stations (Madison has excellent coverage)
  • Local crime rates (generally low in Madison)
  • Weather risks (tornadoes, hail, severe storms)
  • Your credit score and claims history
  • New construction vs. established homes
  • Commute distance to Research Park or Huntsville
