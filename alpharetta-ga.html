Frequently Asked Questions - Alpharetta GA

Do I need flood insurance in Alpharetta, GA? +

Properties near Big Creek or the Chattahoochee River face increased flood risk. The Big Creek floodplain and areas with poor drainage are particularly vulnerable during heavy rains. Standard homeowners policies don't cover flood damage, so separate flood insurance is recommended for at-risk properties.

What factors affect my insurance rates in Alpharetta? +
