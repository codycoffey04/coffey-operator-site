Frequently Asked Questions - ALpharetta GA Insurance

What's the minimum auto insurance required in Georgia? +

Georgia requires minimum liability coverage of 25/50/25, which means:

  • $25,000 bodily injury per person
  • $50,000 bodily injury per accident
  • $25,000 property damage

However, most ALpharetta GA residents choose higher limits (100/300/100) for better protection, especially with increasing medical costs and vehicle values.

Do I need flood insurance in ALpharetta GA, GA? +

It depends on your location. Properties near Big Creek or in low-lying areas near the Chattahoochee River may benefit from flood coverage. Even if not required, we recommend it for many ALpharetta GA homes due to our heavy rainfall patterns. Standard homeowners policies don't cover flood damage.
