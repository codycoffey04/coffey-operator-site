Do I need flood insurance in Mountain Brook, AL? +

It depends on your property's location. Properties near the Cahaba River or in low-lying areas may require flood insurance. Even if not required, we often recommend it due to Alabama's heavy rainfall patterns. Standard homeowners policies don't cover flood damage, so this separate coverage is essential for complete protection.

FAQ(this)"> What's the minimum auto insurance required in Alabama? +

Alabama requires minimum liability coverage of 25/50/25, which means:

  • $25,000 bodily injury per person
  • $50,000 bodily injury per accident
  • $25,000 property damage

However, most Mountain Brook residents choose higher limits (100/300/100 or even 250/500/250) for better protection, especially given the higher property values and liability exposures in our area.
